About me

I’m a designer and illustrator graduated from University of Valparaíso, currently living in Viña del Mar, Chile. I’m a person that likes to constantly learn new skills and techniques, a quality that got stronger after becoming a designer.

Since a very young age, i’ve always been interested in illustration and ways to express my creativity, however studying design helped me focus and control that creativity towards solutions, and visual experiences.

what are my strenghts?

// Self-Driven, Self-taught

I’m always looking for new skills or knowledge to earn, and i see each project as an opportunity to do so. If I find an obstacle or something im not familiar with during my work, I get to investigating to at least understand it to a level that allows me to communicate with some one with more experience in the matter.

// Team player

Due to the nature of my discipline, i’m used to work with professionals from different fields, which allows me to obtain new points of view and knowledge to solve every problem.

// Perfectionist

I like to see each project to completion, at a point in which I’m happy with the result, always looking for ways to improve every detail, taking every precaution to do so without forcing either myself or others, by planning and organizing my flow of work.
