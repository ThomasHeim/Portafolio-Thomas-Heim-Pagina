Careta

Design of game components and packaginga

Client: Niebla Games

Work made for “Careta”, the first board game published by Niebla Games, based around the universe of their videogame “Causa: Voices of the Dusk”. I was tasked with the design of all of the components of the game, except for the main box illustration and the characters in the cards, which were made by the studio’s visual artist beforehand.

Leyendas de los Otori

University project

Hard cover edition redesign of the book “Legends of the Otori” as part of my university projects. Except fot the manuscript, all the content was designed from scratch digitally, adding graphic elements alluding to the contents of the story, which then was turned into a mock-up made by hand.
